Update function necessary for all visualization classes.

This function updates the subplot displaying positions and crown radius of all individual trees. Hereby, the distinct tree groups are indicated by varying colors. The Plotsize is derived from the distribution of trees, such that all tree centers a shown.

Parameters
tree_groups- list of tree groups as processes by Manga.
time- double indicating current time
