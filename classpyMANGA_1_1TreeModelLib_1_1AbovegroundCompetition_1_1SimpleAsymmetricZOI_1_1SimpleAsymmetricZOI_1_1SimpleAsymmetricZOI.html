This functions prepares arrays for the competition concept.

In the SimpleAssymmetricZOI concept, trees geometric measures are saved in simple lists and the timestepping is updated. Two numpy arrays similar to the mesh array for canopy height and tree ID of highest tree at corresponding mesh point are initialised.

Parameters
t_ini- initial time for next timestep
t_end- end time for next timestep
